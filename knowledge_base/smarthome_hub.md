# SmartHome Connect Hub (Gen 3) User Guide

## Overview
The SmartHome Connect Hub is the central brain for your automated home. It integrates with over 500 devices from various brands using Zigbee, Z-Wave, and Wi-Fi.

## Setup Instructions
1. Plug the Hub into a power outlet.
2. Connect the Hub to your router via Ethernet or set up Wi-Fi using the SmartHome App.
3. Once the LED light turns solid green, you can start adding devices.

## Features
- **Voice Control Integration:** Works with Alexa and Google Assistant.
- **Automation Engine:** Create "Scenes" and "Routines" to automate lights, locks, and climate.
- **Remote Access:** Control your home from anywhere in the world via the SmartHome App.

## Troubleshooting
- **Hub Offline:** Check your internet connection and ensure the Ethernet cable is securely plugged in. Restart the Hub by unplugging it for 30 seconds.
- **Device Not Pairing:** Ensure the device is in pairing mode and is within 30 feet of the Hub or a repeater.
- **LED Error Codes:** Solid Red indicates a hardware failure. Blinking Blue indicates the Hub is in setup mode.
