# TechGizmo Pro X1 User Manual

## Overview
The TechGizmo Pro X1 is a high-performance tablet designed for creative professionals. It features a 12.9-inch Liquid Retina XDR display, the M2 chip, and support for the TechStylus Gen 2.

## Technical Specifications
- **Processor:** Octa-core M2 Chip with 10-core GPU.
- **Storage:** Options range from 128GB to 2TB.
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, and optional 5G.
- **Battery Life:** Up to 10 hours of web surfing or video playback.

## Troubleshooting
- **Unresponsive Screen:** Force restart by pressing and quickly releasing the volume up button, then the volume down button, and then pressing and holding the power button until the logo appears.
- **Battery Draining Fast:** Check background app activity and brightness levels in Settings.
- **Connectivity Issues:** Reset network settings or verify that Airplane Mode is OFF.

## Warranty Information
TechGizmo Pro X1 comes with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Standard warranty does not cover accidental damage.
