# CloudSaaS Enterprise Service Guide

## Overview
CloudSaaS Enterprise is a comprehensive B2B software-as-a-service platform providing tools for project management, team collaboration, and resource planning.

## Key Modules
- **ProjectX:** Advanced Kanban boards and Gantt charts for project tracking.
- **Connective:** Real-time chat and video conferencing for teams.
- **ResourcePool:** HR and finance module for managing assets and payroll.

## Support Tiers
- **Standard:** 24/5 email support with a 24-hour response time.
- **Business:** 24/7 email and chat support with a 4-hour response time.
- **Enterprise:** 24/7 priority support with a dedicated account manager and 1-hour response time.

## API Integration
CloudSaaS provides a RESTful API for integrating with third-party applications. Authentication is handled via OAuth 2.0.

## Troubleshooting
- **Login Issues:** Verify your credentials and ensure your subscription is active. Contact your organization's administrator for SSO issues.
- **Data Not Syncing:** Check your network connection. Clear browser cache or try an Incognito window.
- **Performance Lag:** Review large data sets or complex filters that may slow down processing.

## Billing and Warranty
Billing is handled on a seat-based subscription model. Service Level Agreement (SLA) guarantees 99.9% uptime.
